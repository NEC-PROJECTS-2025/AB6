1. Precision

Definition: Precision measures how many of the predicted positive results are actually correct.

Formula:

Precision = TP / (TP + FP)

2. Recall

Definition: Recall measures how many of the actual positive results are correctly identified by the system.

Formula:

Recall = TP / (TP + FN)

3. F1-Score

Definition: F1-score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, providing a balanced evaluation.

Formula:

F1-Score = 2 × (Precision × Recall) / (Precision + Recall)

4. Accuracy

Definition: Accuracy measures the proportion of total predictions that were correct.

Formula:

Accuracy = (TP + TN) / Total Predictions
